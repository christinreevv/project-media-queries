Monster High School

Monster High is a multimedia-supported doll franchise from Mattel that was launched in 2010. Its story revolves around the terrific teenage descendants of the world's most famous monsters as they brave the trials and tribulations of a mixed-creature high school in a world that's barely ready for that. Making a few enemies and lots of friends along the way, the students find their way through trouble both big and small.
